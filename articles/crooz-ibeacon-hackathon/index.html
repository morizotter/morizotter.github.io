I won CROOZ Ibeacon Hackathon

2014-06-30

I won the Grand Prix in the hackathon held in CROOZ office on Jun 28th.

  • テックヒルズ×iBeacon ハッカソン【ハンズオンも同時開催】

The hackathon is to make an iOS application using iBeacon installed in various places in CROOZ office in one day. Finally, I ran around CROOZ and made a 3 and a half minutes demo movie.

It was easy to detect a beacon, but the detection rate is bad. So, I changed the implementation slightly. I registered the regions around all the beacons at the start of beacon detection, and detect all beacon when entering the region area. This improvement made detection rate up. This was implemented just 30 minutes before the end. After that, we ran and took a movie.
