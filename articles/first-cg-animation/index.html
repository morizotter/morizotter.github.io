CG animation: CITY 3B6FA

2018-08-31

CITY 3B6FA

CITY 3B6FA is my first try to make a full CG animation. It describes a future city.

The end of July, I decided an outlook of the world and in the first 2 weeks of August, I created a storyboard and character design. From the 3rd week of August, I began work on modelling and animation. That time, I always work for a daily job and CG except for sleep time.

Impressions

I made a full animation work for the first time with Maya and AfterEffects. I wanted to give a more realistic, dark feeling, but sometimes it is immature. However, I think that I would like to keep doing CG work.
