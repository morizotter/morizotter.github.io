I won the Grand Prix in Qiita Hackathon

2013-11-30

I won the Qiita hackathon held on Nov. 9th. You can see the scene in Qiita Blog. As a prize, I got iPad mini Retina Display model and Amazon gift card 10,000yen.

At that day, the camera of WBS(World Business Satellite), Japanese business news broadcasting service was there, so, I debuted to the tv news. Because it was my first hackathon, I was very nervous and I didn’t know it succeeds until the end. I was really happy to complete the product and won the Grand Prix. I was also impressed by the participants.
